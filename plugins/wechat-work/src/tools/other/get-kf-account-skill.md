# Get kf account (`wechat-work-get-kf-account`)

Calls `GET /cgi-bin/kf/account/get` to get details of a customer service account.

## Parameters

- **wechat_work_credential** (required): `credential_id` for `wechat-work`.
- **account_id** (required): Customer service account ID.

## Output

Returns account details including id, name, avatar, status, and wx.
